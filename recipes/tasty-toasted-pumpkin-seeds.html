Tasty Toasted Pumpkin Seeds

Description

This recipe is for flavorful, crisp, toasted pumpkin seeds. They are a technically a healthy snack, but surprisingly addictive and munchable!

Ingredients

  • The seeds from a regular carving or pie pumpkin.
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp ground sea salt (or more, to taste)
  • Canola oil cooking spray

Steps

  1. Scoop pumpkin seeds from pumpkin and rinse clean.A colandar or strainer can be helpful here.
  2. Spread the pumpkin seeds on a kitchen towel to air dry. Do not use a paper towel because it will stick to the seeds.
  3. Spread pumpkin seeds on a foil-lined cookie sheet.
  4. Spray lightly with canola oil cooking spray.
  5. Sprinkle with cumin powder, paprika, and sea salt.
  6. Bake at 300 degrees F for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
