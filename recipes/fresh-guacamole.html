Fresh Guacamole

Description

This recipe is for a very simple, quick, and easy to make guacamole.

Ingredients

  • Three avocadoes
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 Tbsp. mayonnaise
  • Two Tbsp. green taco sauce

Steps

  1. Rinse avocadoes, peel, and mash.
  2. Mix in mayonnaise, mashing in with a fork.
  3. Mix in green taco sauce.
  4. Add pinch of salt and lemon juice.
  5. Stir and mash with fork until desired smoothness.
  6. Enjoy!
